02  /  How We Approach It

Problem First. Solution Second.

Most clients don't arrive with a defined problem — they arrive with a feeling that something could be better. That's exactly where we start.

  • 1

    We learn your situation, your business, and what you're trying to achieve. You don't need to know what the solution looks like before we talk.

  • 2

    The discovery call is free. The written proposal is free. You receive a tailored scope, cost, and timeline before spending a dollar — no obligation, no pressure. If it's not the right fit, keep it.

  • 3

    Every job is bespoke. We don't sell packages — we solve specific problems for specific people. The solution is built around you.

  • 4

    If scope changes mid-engagement, we stop, re-scope, re-quote, and get written acceptance before continuing. No silent creep. No surprises on the invoice.

05  /  Ownership

What's Not Included — And Why That's the Point

We don't mark up third-party costs. We don't lock you into our infrastructure. Every solution is built for you to own outright — permanently.

Third-Party LLM Credits

OpenAI, Anthropic, Google — API costs are billed directly to you at cost. No markup. No dependency on us to keep the service running.

Hosting & Hardware

Compute, servers, and hosting are yours — not ours. We favour solutions that run on infrastructure you already control or can set up independently.

Why we do it this way

We believe in localised, sustainable ownership. The goal of every engagement is to leave you with a solution that runs independently — no ongoing fees flowing through us, no single point of failure, no lock-in. When the job is done, the solution is yours entirely.

That said — if you'd rather we hold the hardware on your behalf, we can explore that avenue.
