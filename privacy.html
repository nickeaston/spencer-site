Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 April 2026 · Effective date: pending

Spencer is a service of Easton Enterprises, an Australian sole-trader business operating from Victoria, Australia. We take the privacy of personal information seriously and handle it in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. Who we are

In this policy, "we", "us" and "our" refer to Easton Enterprises (the entity behind the Spencer service). You can reach us at welcome@myspencer.ai. Our primary place of business is Melbourne, Victoria, Australia.

2. What information we collect

We only collect personal information that is reasonably necessary for the services we provide. Depending on how you interact with us, this may include:

  • Contact details: name, email address, phone number, business name.
  • Booking information: when you book a discovery call via Cal.com — your selected time, time zone, any answers you provide to intake questions, and the booking confirmation record.
  • Engagement information: if you become a client, information you choose to share about your business, workflows, accounts, or processes so we can build Spencer for you.
  • Technical and analytics data: standard web analytics provided by Cloudflare Web Analytics (cookieless), which records aggregate visit counts, page views, referrers, country-level location and browser type. We do not use cookies for tracking.
  • Correspondence: the content of emails, messages or chat exchanges you send us.

3. How we collect it

  • Directly from you — when you book a call, email us, or share information during an engagement.
  • Automatically — limited, aggregate analytics when you visit this website.
  • Through your own connected systems — if, during a paid engagement, you authorise Spencer to access your Google Workspace, Telegram, calendar or other accounts via their official APIs, we receive only what those APIs return for the scope you grant. You can revoke that access at any time from inside those services.

4. Why we collect it (purpose)

  • To respond to enquiries and conduct discovery calls.
  • To deliver paid Spencer engagements you have agreed to.
  • To send service-related communications (e.g. booking confirmations, prep notes, project updates).
  • To improve our website and the quality of our services.
  • To comply with our legal and regulatory obligations.

5. Who we share it with

We do not sell personal information. We share it only with trusted service providers who help us run Spencer, and only to the extent necessary. These currently include:

  • Cal.com — booking platform (USA / EU).
  • Cloudflare — DNS, content delivery and privacy-first web analytics (USA / global edge).
  • GitHub Pages — static website hosting (USA).
  • Google Workspace — email and calendar (USA / global).
  • Anthropic — provider of the Claude AI models used to power Spencer's automations (USA).
  • Telegram — messaging delivery for client deliverables, where the client opts in (UAE / global).

Each of these providers operates under their own privacy terms. We choose providers we consider reputable and require them to handle information responsibly, but we cannot control their internal practices.

6. Overseas disclosure (APP 8)

Because the providers listed above are based outside Australia, your personal information may be stored or processed overseas — primarily in the United States. By using Spencer or providing us with personal information, you consent to that overseas disclosure. We take reasonable steps to satisfy ourselves that those providers handle personal information consistently with the APPs, but APP 8.1 may not apply where you have given this consent.

7. How we store and protect it

We hold information in encrypted-at-rest cloud services (Google Workspace, GitHub, Cloudflare) and on a controlled-access workstation in Melbourne. We use strong passwords, multi-factor authentication where available, and limit access to information to what is needed to deliver our services. No system is perfectly secure; if a data breach occurs that is likely to result in serious harm, we will comply with the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme.

8. How long we keep it

We keep personal information only for as long as we need it for the purposes set out above, or as required by law (for example, the Australian Taxation Office record-keeping rules typically require us to retain financial records for at least five years). When information is no longer needed, we delete or de-identify it.

9. Your rights — access and correction (APP 12 & 13)

You can ask us at any time to:

  • Tell you what personal information we hold about you.
  • Provide a copy of it.
  • Correct it if it is inaccurate, out of date or incomplete.
  • Delete it, where we are not required to keep it.

Email welcome@myspencer.ai with the subject line "Privacy request". We aim to respond within 30 days. There is no fee for making a request, although we may charge a reasonable cost for retrieving voluminous historical records.

10. Cookies and tracking

This website uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is cookieless and does not fingerprint individual users. We do not use third-party advertising, retargeting pixels, or behavioural-tracking cookies. The Cal.com booking widget embedded in our Book section may set its own cookies necessary for the booking flow — those are governed by Cal.com's own privacy notice.

11. Children

Spencer is a professional service intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it.

12. AI processing

Spencer's automations use the Claude AI models provided by Anthropic. When you are an active client and you authorise Spencer to act on your data, that data may be sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic's commercial terms (which we operate under) state that customer inputs and outputs are not used to train their models by default. We do not pool client data across clients, and we do not use one client's data to train, fine-tune or improve services for other clients.

13. Complaints

If you believe we have breached your privacy, please raise it with us first by emailing welcome@myspencer.ai. We take complaints seriously and will investigate promptly. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au or by phone on 1300 363 992.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent change. Material changes will be notified to active clients by email.
